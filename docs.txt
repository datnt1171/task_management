Project Overview
	What: Task Management system which follow fixed ISO process
	Who its for: Internal company use

	Tech stack:
	Frontend: Nextjs, Tailwind, Axios
	Backend: Django, DRF
	Auth: JWT
	Database: PostgreSQL
